Hero Of The Land

A game by Thurston Beers.

Backround

I made this game so that I could have a game to play on long car rides.

A long time ago in the land of cards an evil sorcerer cast a spell over the lands this spell turned almost everyone highly combustable zombies only two royal jokers and a few others remain its youre job to save the royalty because there the only ones who can make the anti-spell.

What you need:

  • Deck of playing cards
  • Skill
  • IQ

Game Rules:

  1. Shuffle the deck of cards.
  2. Set the deck of cards into the deck box face down.
  3. Draw five cards from the top of the deck these are your starting cards you may look at them.
