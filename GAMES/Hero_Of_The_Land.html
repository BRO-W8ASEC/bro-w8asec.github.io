CAR(DS)

A game by Thurston Beers.

Backround

I made this game so that I could have a game to play on long car rides.

A long time ago in the land of cards an evil sorcerer cast a spell over the lands this spell turned almost everyone into highly combustable zombies only two royal jokers and a few others remain its youre job to save the royalty because there the only ones who can make the anti-spell.

What you need:

  • Deck of playing cards
  • Skill
  • IQ

Game Rules:

  1. Shuffle the deck of cards.
  2. Set the deck of cards into the deck box face down.
  3. Draw five cards from the top of the deck these are your starting cards you may look at them.
  4. The attack phase is next draw one card from the top of the deck lets say that the card that you drew is the five of spades you can beat this card with a higher power card (6,7,8,9,10,ace) with another five or by or by useing two or more cards that are equal to or more than the value of the card you are fighting if you beat the card you put it into your hand but put the card(s) you used to defeat it at the bottom of the deck if you lose you keep the card you were fighting but lose a heart (if you cant or dont want to beat it you lose a heart but keep the card).
  5. You start with three hearts if you lose a heart it can not be recoverd.
  6. If you draw a Joker put it into you hand Jokers allow you to pick up royal cards and put them into your hand if they match the color of your Joker(s).
  7. If you get all four sets of royalty you win but if you lose all of you hearts you lose.
